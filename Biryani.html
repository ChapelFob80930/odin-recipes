Biryani

Ingredients:

  1. 1 kg chicken
  2. 1 kg basmati rice
  3. 1 cup yogurt
  4. 1 tbsp ginger paste
  5. 1 tbsp garlic paste
  6. 1 tbsp red chili powder
  7. 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  8. 1 tbsp garam masala
  9. 1 tbsp cumin powder
  10. 1 tbsp coriander powder
  11. 1 tbsp chaat masala
  12. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  13. 1 tbsp mustard oil
  14. 1 tbsp salt
  15. 1 tbsp black salt
  16. 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  17. 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Wash the chicken and rice.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for 2 hours.
  5. Boil the rice in a pot.
  6. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot.
  7. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove the pot from the heat and serve hot.
