Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients:

  1. 1 kg chicken
  2. 1 cup yogurt
  3. 1 tbsp ginger paste
  4. 1 tbsp garlic paste
  5. 1 tbsp red chili powder
  6. 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  7. 1 tbsp garam masala
  8. 1 tbsp cumin powder
  9. 1 tbsp coriander powder
  10. 1 tbsp chaat masala
  11. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  12. 1 tbsp mustard oil
  13. 1 tbsp salt
  14. 1 tbsp black salt
  15. 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  16. 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Wash the chicken and make slits on it.
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for 2 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  5. Place the chicken on a baking tray and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Turn the chicken over and bake for another 30 minutes.
  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and serve hot.
